Interactive Audio for Products & Hardware

This section showcases my experience in designing sound for tangible products and systems where audio is integral to the user experience. My work spans from health-tech hardware to large-scale public infrastructure, focusing on Human-Centered Design, accessibility, and innovative applications of haptic technology.

Health-Tech Rhythm Game

Merry Electronic

Led the development of a patent-pending, music-based rhythm game integrated into a hardware product to combat sarcopenia in the elderly. Primary inventor on the patent.

5G Haptic Wearable Vest

Taiwan Sound Lab, C-LAB

Led the "Feel Together" project, a wearable vest that translates sound into nuanced haptic vibrations for cultural accessibility. Also designed haptic sensations for a 5G sensory wearable concert and workshops.

Psychoacoustic Alarm System

French National Railway (SNCF)

Spearheaded the design of a system-wide alarm based on psychoacoustics, now deployed on vehicles. The research was published and presented at WCRR 2022.
